# Test Strategy

## Objective

Ensure reliability and accuracy of financial and inventory transactions in the POS system.

## Test Priorities

Priority 1 – Critical Transactions
- Checkout
- Payment processing
- Refund handling
- Inventory updates

Priority 2 – Medium Risk
- Add item to cart
- Discount calculation
- Tax calculation

Priority 3 – Low Risk
- Barcode scanning
- Receipt formatting

## Testing Focus

### Transaction Integrity
Verify that transactions are recorded correctly.

### Payment Reliability
Ensure that successful payments always result in completed transactions.

### Inventory Consistency
Ensure inventory levels update correctly after sales and refunds.
