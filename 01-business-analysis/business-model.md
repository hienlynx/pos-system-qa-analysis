# Business Model – POS System

A Point of Sale system is used by retail businesses to process sales transactions and manage inventory.

## Core Workflow

Product selection  
↓  
Cart creation  
↓  
Checkout  
↓  
Payment processing  
↓  
Receipt generation  
↓  
Inventory update

## Business Objectives

- Record sales transactions accurately
- Process customer payments reliably
- Maintain accurate inventory levels
- Generate receipts for completed transactions
